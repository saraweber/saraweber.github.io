About Me

Just a couple of things

As you may have guessed, my name is Sara. I attended Texas A&M University and received a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Visualization. I took one class for designing for the web and instantly knew that I had found what I wanted to use my CS degree for. I'm constantly working to learn more about this field and practice using what i've learned through little projects of my own.

I believe that the key to a good product is not only great design but usability and functionality, a product can be beautiful but if it lacks functionality then the product is useless. I have worked with HTML/CSS and have experience with using Javascript. I am also familiar with the software development lifecycle and have industry experience using both the agile and scrum methodologies.
